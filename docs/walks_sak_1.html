Sakkara 1 Area

Stonehenge area

  • Jump up the pieces of the Stonehenge structure and floorslam the switch on the final monument platform. Now the Atun disc is turned on nearby.
  • Go past the atun disc , the lever behind a bamboo wall and keep going until you are stopped by a bamboo door. Hit the switch above the door to open it and progress past the door.

Bridges area

  • In this area there is a container on the floor, break it open to collect a statue head.
  • In this area there is a container on a tall column, lure the nearby armadillo into ramming the tower, the tower shakes and the item container falls off and smashes on the ground revealing a statue head, collect it.

Stonehenge area

  • Go back to the lever behind the bamboo wall
  • There are 2 headless statues either side of it, use the heads on them to lower the bamboo wall and gain access to the lever.
  • Pull the lever to open the big door up the slope in this area (temporarily), quickly run onto the Atun Disc to get the temporary speed up ability. Use the speed to get through the open door before it closes.
  • Pick up a small wooden raft and throw it onto the acid, use the platform to jump to the other side.
  • Use the block as a stepping stone to get to the other side.
  • Use an slim burble to break open the debris in front of the cave.
  • Progress through the cave to the ropes
  • Jump from rope to rope in the cave to progress through it.

Swamp

Forwards

  • Go down to the first spitting toad, hit the nearby tree to get some Sakkarian berries. Use the toad it to spit the player on the high ledge which has a zipline. The toads will only be active if you have sakkarian berries in your inventory.
  • Use the Zipline and to get to the opposite platform, then jump down onto the rock platform below in the middle of poisonous water.
  • EXTRA or ride the zipline to the far side. Climb up the vines to get to a high platform. Use slim burble on the nearby debris to receive a reward.
  • Shoot an ice dart into the fire eye of the nearby statue. This causes tilting wooden platforms to temporarily appear.
  • Jump across the tilting platforms to get across to the other side.
  • Go to the nearby spitting toad and get spat across near to the possession point.
  • There are some wooden platforms leading up a hill. Use them to reach the top.
  • Once at the top, talk to the farmer. He will offer you a medicinal rootâ€”buy one, as it will be needed later.
  • After purchasing it, go back down using the same wooden platforms.
  • Use the nearby toad to get spat to the next platform along.
  • Talk to the old man and offer him the medicinal root you bought earlier. As a reward, he will give you a glyph key.
  • Jump up the ledge.
  • Shoot the fire eye of the statue with an ice dart to temporarily create wooden tilter platforms to gain access to back to the start of the swamp area.
  • Go back towards the Stonehenge area

Backwards

  • Go to the nearby spitting toad and get spat across near to the possession point.
  • QUEST If the player has the possession scroll they can use the nearby possession point to possess the nearby frog.
    • Once the player is a frog he can jump up the nearby high platforms and work his way into the center structure of the swamp using his high jump to negotiate the platforms.
    • Once at the top the player can use the unpossession point to transform back into Sphinx.
    • Nearby there is a special leafy tree, hit this to get the LEAF quest item used to build a geb trap in Sakkara 7.

Stonehenge area

  • There is a nearby bamboo door with an eagle-shaped key pedestal nearby the wall. Set the glyph key into it to make the door drop open.
  • Pick up the statue and progress down the corridor and put it on the shrine at the end to keep the next door open.

Bridges area

  • Go along the bridge and shoot the 2 fire eyes with an ice dart to cause the statue to drop a rope down.
  • Climb up the rope and jump across to the next platform.
  • Shoot the spider on the wall opposite.
  • Pull the chain to temporarily open the stone bridge in this area. Quickly get to the bridge and jump across to the other side.
  • Use a slim burble to break open the debris in front of the cave entrance. Go through the opening to reach Sakkara 8
