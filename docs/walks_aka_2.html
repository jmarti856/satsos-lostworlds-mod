Akaria 2

Kings Throne Room

  • From the start of the level run forwards and up over the first little bridge. Now head left over the next one a cut-scene will now take over.
  • Once over the cut-scene is over drop down into the water, in here you will find 4-pressure switches and 4 blocks. By placing one of the blocks on each of the presser switches you will light one of the troches above. When all four have been lit then a chest will appear once opened this will transport you to chapter to of the Mummy levels.

After completing Mummy chapter 2.

  • Once the cut-scene has ended head through the door Ishka went through.
