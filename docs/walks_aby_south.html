Main Quest

  • (1) Player enters area and goes around corner
  • (3+4) Player meets with 3 upper class people who tell him to lower the bridge for them (-45.92 -7.80 -5.64)
  • (11) Guard tells player about missing lever and where to get a new one (player must go to Abydos North to get the lever) (-16.05 -3.85 -40.85)

Go to Aby Nrth, triggering the following upon completing objectives there.

  • When coming back from Abydos North the player will be chasing the thief who stole the lever (Orange dots)
  • When the player has blocked the path of the thief (by pulling the lever to close the wooden gate at the top of the market area) he will get hold of the lever.
  • (A) Player can use the 3 levers in this house to lower the bridges (B and C) (-16.05 -3.85 -40.85)
  • Player can enter council houses
  • Halfway across the bridge will be a guard blocking the players path, the player must get around him by going around the outside of the structure using the grababble ledge on the right hand side (-90.91 -7.31 -19.28)
  • Continue along the bridge until you enter the Council houses, where the mayor is to be found.

Return from Council houses, with the quest to locate five herbs for the mayor.

  • Herbs are all located on the pier house, 1 herb is bought in Abydos North (others to be spread out over the city later) (-62.38 -13. 27 -59.03)
  • Once player has all the herbs he returns to council houses.
  • Mayor is saved so the player can now make his way to Akaria (D) by using the diving bell
